ACM Annual Event

ACM Annual Event for the year 2023 was hosted by the Satpuda College of Information & Bio Technology, Sausar, on the 11th of February 2023. ACM, the Association for Computing Machinery is the worlds largest educational and scientific society, uniting computing educators, researchers and professionals to inspire dialogue, share resources and address the fields challenges. ACM recognizes excellence in computer science and information technology through a host of prestigious awards, including the Turing Award (aka Nobel Prize in Computing). ACM supports the professional growth of its members by providing opportunities for life-long learning, career development, and professional networking. ARCS 2023 and the ACM-W India Decade Celebration event were co-located with ACM India annual event.

TechAcme2k23

TechAcme is a annual Techno-Cultural Fest of Satpuda College of Information & Bio Technology that comprised of events covering diversified arenas of culture, technology and art. TechACME2k23 included various fascinating technical events liks LAN Gaming, RoboRace, Talent Start Light, Assemble Disassemble, Project Presentation, Circuit designing Traditional Fashion Show and many more. The grand fiesta evening by Rashmeet Kaur , the celebrity singer, mesmerized gathering of more than 2500 audience with her songs. Knowledge exchange, fun and experiential learning happened throughout at large scale. The inaugural ceremony of Tech Acme took place on 6 April’23, in the OIST Auditorium. The programme was successfully conducted under the chief hospitality of Prof(Dr.) Sunil Kumar, Vice Chancellor, Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya, Bhopal along with Guest of honour, Dr. Shrikant Patil, MD, CRISP, Bhopal. The program witnessed the gracious presence of Shri Jaywardhan Joshi, Co-Convenor, Professional Cell BJP, Shri Praveen Thakral Chairman, Satpuda Group of Institutes.
